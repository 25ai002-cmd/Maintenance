Society Rules & Regulations

1. Maintenance Payment

All residents must pay monthly maintenance before the due date mentioned in the dashboard. If maintenance is not paid within 5 days after the due date, then a fine of ₹250 will be added to the pending maintenance amount.

2. Parking Rules

Vehicles must only be parked in designated parking areas. Illegal parking may result in penalties.

3. Noise Restrictions

Loud music and disturbances are prohibited after 10 PM to maintain peaceful surroundings.

4. Cleanliness

Residents must keep common areas clean and avoid throwing garbage outside designated zones.

5. Security

Visitors should be registered at the security gate before entering the society premises.
